Abstract

This project presents a food delivery web application oriented to offer a catering service to companies and their employees. Unlike other food delivery platforms, its orientation will be the delivery of food in the office, grouping all the orders of employees. This platform would provide any company with the ability to offer an "a la carte" food service with delivery, in addition to significant financial savings and staff retention.

The application uses a Progressive Web Application (PWA) architecture based on the Angular platform, using recent versions of CSS and HTML, and REST API services for data encapsulation.

For its development we will apply the methodologies acquired in this master and agile methods, such as Kanban boards for the management of tasks and their lifecycle. The Mobile First method will allow us to obtain a product correctly adapted to mobile devices.

Finally, an analysis of the results obtained, compared to the initial objectives, and an estimation of future opportunities.
